# FAQ

**Refund window**: 90 days.
**Shipping delays**: standard 5–7 business days; international 10–20 days.
**Damaged items**: submit photos within 14 days for replacement or refund.
**Billing errors**: contact support with your order ID.

This FAQ is intentionally broad and can become dominant in retrieval if chunking is poor.

Common questions:
- Can I change my shipping address after ordering? Only before fulfillment; use the shipping portal.
- Do you ship to EU? Yes; customs can add 3–5 days.
- When do credits apply? At checkout; cannot combine with post-purchase refunds.
- What happens after 120 days? Refunds generally denied unless consumer law requires exceptions.
