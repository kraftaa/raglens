# Refund Policy (Current)

Customers may request a refund within 90 days of purchase.
Refunds after 90 days require manager approval and a ticket in the billing system.
Refunds for damaged goods are always honored with photo evidence.

Steps:
1. Locate order in billing system.
2. Verify purchase date and amount.
3. Approve refund if within 90 days; escalate if beyond.

Edge cases:
- Subscription refunds: prorate unused period.
- Bundled items: refund only bundle price, not individual list prices.
- Fraudulent charge: route to risk; do not refund until investigation closes.

Notes:
- Always record the refund reason code (R1: defect, R2: late delivery, R3: customer remorse).
- Manager approval required for refunds over $500 or beyond 90 days.
