# Shipping Policy

Shipping options include standard (5–7 business days) and express (2–3 business days).
International shipping may take 10 to 20 days and can be delayed by customs.
Returns for lost packages require a claim after 14 days past the expected delivery date.

SLA targets:
- US standard: 95% delivered within 7 business days.
- US express: 95% delivered within 3 business days.
- EU: 90% delivered within 12 business days.

Restrictions:
- Hazardous materials cannot be shipped express.
- P.O. boxes allow only standard shipping.
